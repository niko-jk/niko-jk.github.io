Ev_ Naga_ was born in Silver Spring, Maryland, and first educated amidst the DIY dissidents of the nation's capital and its suburban discontents. She received a B.A. in Sociology from the Quakers at Guilford College in Greensboro, North Carolina, and continued her early education traveling in Mexico where she studied social movement theory with an emphasis on art and autonomy. Eva is the full-time Director of the Exoneration Project, a pro bono legal clinic at the University of Chicago, and the part-time host of a new MTV documentary series, "Unlocking the Truth."
